About the Simply Steel Team

Kishor (Kesh) Chhiba was introduced to forging when he was an apprentice for William Cables (Wellington) over 38 years ago – that makes him pretty old in today’s fast paced world! But it truly shows his passion for forging. He continues to create wonderful designs for his clients with the same passion today.

Mike Enoka has been working with Kesh for over 10 years, his attention to detail is sometimes overwhelming, but he gets it spot on every time! His ability to create living pieces of wrought iron work is truly amazing.

Marlti (Marti) Chhiba is the stay at work house wife! She will most likely be your first point of contact at our showroom, where she can help you start your project whether it be a gate, balustrade, a piece of furniture, garden product or simply a work of art you want made from metal.

Simply New Zealand...SimplySteel is proud to be Wellington based and a ‘New Zealand made’ business. The company was established in 1990 with the goal of providing unique, high quality wrought iron pieces. SimplySteel has established a reputation in Wellington and throughout New Zealand for superior quality and workmanship.


With more than 20 years’ experience…For over three decades, SimplySteel has been synonymous with the highest quality wrought iron Gates, Fencing, Balustrades, Hand Rails, custom Furniture and other made-to-order products. Today, we continue to proudly deliver excellent standards of craftsmanship together with our famous personal service and value for money.
